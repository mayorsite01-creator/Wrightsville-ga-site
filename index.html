A Clear Agenda for Wrightsville

The Strategic Vision

Three focused pillars built to make Wrightsville a stronger, more connected, and more prosperous city — for every resident and every family in Johnson County.

Community First

Pillar One

Mayor Outlaw is committed to governance that puts Johnson County's families at the center of every decision. From neighborhood improvements to youth investment, the Office of the Mayor is a partner — not just a regulator — in Wrightsville's daily life.

Economic Development

Pillar Two

Building a stronger Wrightsville means creating conditions where local businesses can grow and new employers want to invest. Mayor Outlaw is dedicated to reducing barriers, developing infrastructure, and positioning Johnson County as an attractive destination for opportunity.

Modern City Services

Pillar Three

Every resident deserves city services that are accessible and efficient. Mayor Outlaw is bringing Wrightsville's government into the digital age — modernizing how residents pay, report, and connect with their city without losing the small-community responsiveness that sets Wrightsville apart.

Rooted in Wrightsville.
